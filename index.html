Taco Tuesday

Tacos

Ingredients List

  • Chili Powder - 2 TSP
  • Cumin - 2 TSP
  • Garlic Powder - 1/2 TSP
  • Oregano - 1/2 TSP
  • Salt - 1/2 TSP
  • Black Pepper - 1/2 TSP
  • Ground Beef - 1 LB
  • Tomato Paste/Tomato Sauce - 2 TBSP
  • Olive Oil - 1 TBSP
  • Tortilla/ Corn or Flour
  • Water - 1/2 Cup

Equipment List

  • Non-stick Pan
  • Spatula

Potential Toppings

  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chopped Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Salsa
  • Cilantro
  • Sour Cream
  • Guacamole

Cooking Directions

  1. Heat Olive oil in pan, then add Beef to brown. Approx. 5-7 minutes
  2. Turn to medium-low heat and add spices until sauce has thickend. Approx. 3-5 minutes
  3. Serve onto Tortillas, add toppings as desired
