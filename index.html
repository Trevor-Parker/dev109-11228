Easy Morning Pancakes

Pancakes

Ingredients List

  • All-purpose Flour - 1 1/2 Cup
  • Baking Powder - 3 1/2 TSP
  • White Sugar - 1 TSP
  • Salt - 1/4 TSP
  • Milk - 1 1/4 Cup
  • Butter - 3 TBSP
  • Eggs - 1
  • Syrup of your choice

Equipment List

  • Non-stick Pan
  • Spatula
  • Strainer
  • Whisk
  • Mixing Bowl

Cooking Directions

  1. Sift togethor the Flour, Baking Powder, Sugar, and Salt in the mixing bowl
  2. Add in the Eggs, Milk, and Butter
  3. Put batter in pan and flip after approx 2 minutes
  4. Ensure golden-brown on both sides and serve
