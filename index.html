Taco Tuesday

Tacos

Ingredients List

  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Powder
  • Mexican Oregano
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Paste/Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Tortilla/ Corn or Flour

Equipment List

  • Non-stick Pan
  • Spatula

Potential Toppings

  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Chopped Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Salsa
  • Cilantro
  • Sour Cream
  • Guacamole

Cooking Directions

  1. Heat Olive oil in pan, then add Beef to brown. Approx. 5-7 minutes
  2. Turn to medium-low heat and add spices until sauce has thickend. Approx. 3-5 minutes
  3. Serve onto Tortillas, add toppings as desired
